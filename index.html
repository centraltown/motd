Rules

Hacking/exploiting

- Using any hack that can give an unfair advantage is not allowed
- Any hacks that may steal scripts, config files or any other files is not allowed (Including scripthook)
- Trying to bypass any protections will result in a ban
- Backdoors/backdoor menus are not allowed

General RP rules

- Don't raid the same base over and over again
- 1 terror attack every 30 minutes
- Max mug $5000
- Advert raid before raiding, advert over once done.
- Alts are not allowed
- 1 mug every 20 min

Building rules

- Bases must be raidable (Doors must not be blocked by props, keypads crackable)
- No blackout/whiteout bases
- No maze bases
- No traps that cannot be seen
- You must have a KOS sign to kill players in your base, otherwise it is RDM
- KOS signs must be clearly visible
- You may not shoot through one-ways (unless you are on the solid side)
- Entrances 2 players wide
- No bases on rooftops

Voice/text chat rules

- Don't mic-spam
- Microphone volume has to be at a comfortable level
- Don't spam chat (normal/ooc/advert/pm)
- Inappropriate content (Racism/sexism, sexually explicit material, or links to such material) is not allowed
